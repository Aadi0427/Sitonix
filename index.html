Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI tools and services

General
How long does it take to build a website?
The time it takes depends on the size and complexity of your project. A simple portfolio or business site generally takes around 7 to 10 days, while more advanced projects like eCommerce, multi-page corporate sites, or custom dashboards can take 3 to 5 weeks. We follow a clear process — planning, design, development, testing, and final delivery — and we keep you updated at every step to ensure smooth progress and on-time completion.
Pricing
How much does it cost to use your services?
Our pricing depends on the type of project, design complexity, features, and technologies used — but we always keep it transparent and flexible. We offer custom quotes based on your specific needs. For small websites, prices typically start around 5999, while larger projects can range from 10,000 to 50,000 or more. We also provide maintenance packages and optional add-ons like SEO optimization and content creation. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your project requirements.
Technical
What technologies do you use to build websites?
We use a mix of modern front-end and back-end tools depending on the project. Our stack usually includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js, Tailwind CSS, Node.js, Express, and WordPress. For design, we rely on Figma, Adobe XD, and Canva, and for databases, we use MySQL or MongoDB. We always choose the right tech for your goals — speed, scalability, and security first.
Security
How secure is my data with Sitonix?
We follow strict security protocols during every stage of development. Our process includes: Installing and configuring SSL certificates (HTTPS) Keeping all frameworks, plugins, and dependencies up-to-date Using secure authentication methods for admin access Regularly scanning for malware or vulnerabilities Implementing firewall rules and brute-force protection Your website is built with security as a top priority — not an afterthought.
Support
What kind of support do you provide?
We offer post-launch support to ensure your website runs smoothly. This includes fixing bugs, performance monitoring, and basic maintenance. Extended support plans are also available based on your needs.
Getting Started
How do I start a project with your agency?
Simply fill out the contact form on our website or reach out via email/WhatsApp. Our team will get in touch to understand your goals, requirements, and budget before moving forward. We aim to kick off new projects within a week of initial contact, depending on your readiness and project scope.
